Our Services

Reliable. Experienced. Always Maintained.

We provide various sea freight services with our fleets depending on our clients needs.

Time Chartering


Time Chartering allows you to charter our vessel for a specific period of time. It offers flexibility to transport oils and materials without the need to account every voyage the vessels take in the periods of chartering. We also can provide the type of vessel you need for a specific time for your shipping needs.

Voyage Chartering


Voyage Chartering allows us to charter our vessel to you for a specific voyage or trip. It offers a cost-effective and fast way to transport oils and materials without worrying about charter time. We also can provide the type of vessel you need for a specific trip for your shipping needs.

Handling Agents


We also can provide handling services to assist your seaport operations. These services may include material handling, material handling, ship fueling, and other operational tasks. We also provide flexibility, as you can be hired your handling operation as needed to handle peak periods or special events.
